MIM Theory

The journal of the Maoist Internationalist Movement. Originally only available as scans, but now as fully-searchable and properly-typeset modern PDFs complete with updated graphics and functional PDF navigation.

MIM were the first Maoist group in amerika to take a firm stand against heterosexism, and contributed hugely to Third Worldism, an “alter-globalisation” movement and discourse surrounding the destruction of the First World. The editors do not necessarily endorse everything contained within these journals, but we do think that it provides a valuable point of debate and departure, and are republishing these for their historical value.

The original scans of the documents can be found at the MIM (Prisons) website.
